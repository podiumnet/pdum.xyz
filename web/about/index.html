About Podium

Podium is the social network of everything: We have multiple networking styles for you to use. Additionally, we will supply integrations with other services, such as Twitter, which we actually use for our own promotion with the account @podium_snw.

Our service is entirely, 100% Free Software, licensed under the GNU General Public License, version 3 or later. We understand that rome wasn't built in a day, and not by one person alone. We are a community organization, and accept contributions from anybody who wants to make them! You can do this through our GitHub repository, and can also work on other repositories by our organization. We might even ask you to join us, letting you make contributions without us having to approve of each one!
